Creation Story
In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth. The earth had no shape and was empty. Darkness covered the deep waters. Then God said, 'Let there be light,' and there was light. God saw that the light was good. He separated the light from the darkness. God called the light day and the darkness night. This was the first day.
